About Gerard Gascón

Hello, World!

I'm Gerard Gascón, a young self-taught Game Developer focused mainly on programming.

I have worked on over a dozen game jam games and developed most of my programming skills such as rapid prototyping and also some game design expirience like finding ways to satisfact the player when playing my games.

I have developed other projects in order to further improve my skills aside of Unity Engine, such as programming for the Sega Mega Drive in C or building a Graphics Card from the ground up.

I am currently studying Software Engineering at the Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya and spending my free time in my search for new skills.
